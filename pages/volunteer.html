Be part of the troop!

We are a dedicated and passionate civil association committed to rescuing and rehabilitating horses that have been mistreated, neglected, or abandoned. We rely on the generosity and commitment of volunteers like you to help us achieve our mission.

Volunteering with us is a wonderful way to make a positive difference in the lives of horses in need. Whether you have experience working with horses, or simply a love for animals, there are many ways that you can get involved and help us make a difference.

Some of the volunteer opportunities available include:

  • Horse care and grooming
  • Feeding and watering
  • Barn maintenance and cleaning
  • Fundraising and community outreach
  • Office support
  • Event planning and coordination

No matter what your skills or interests may be, there is a place for you at Horse Care and Rescue. Our friendly and supportive staff will work with you to find a volunteer role that best fits your abilities and schedule.

As a volunteer, you will have the opportunity to:

  • Make a direct and positive impact on the lives of horses in need
  • Learn about horse care and behavior
  • Meet like-minded animal lovers
  • Develop new skills and gain valuable experience
  • Have fun and be part of a supportive community

If you are ready to make a difference and join the fight to save horses in need, we invite you to become a volunteer. To learn more about our volunteer opportunities and to get started, please contact us.

Together, we can make a brighter future for horses everywhere!
