Our mission

We are a dedicated and passionate civil association committed to rescuing and rehabilitating horses in need. Our organization was founded with the goal of providing a safe haven for horses that have been mistreated, neglected, or abandoned, and to raise awareness in society about the mistreatment of equines.

Our team of dedicated volunteers and staff work tirelessly to provide the best possible care for every horse that comes into our care. We provide medical treatment, rehabilitation, and training to help these beautiful creatures recover from their past experiences and find new homes with loving families.

In addition to our rescue and rehabilitation efforts, we are also dedicated to raising awareness about the issue of animal blood traction and equine slaughter. We believe that education and advocacy are key to creating a world in which all horses are treated with kindness, respect, and dignity.

We invite you to join us in our mission. Whether you can donate your time, resources, or simply spread the word about our work, every little bit helps. Together, we can create a brighter future for horses in need. We look forward to working with you to make a positive difference in the lives of horses everywhere!
